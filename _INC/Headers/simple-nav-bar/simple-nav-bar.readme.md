# Simple Nav Bar Component
A simple header that includes a social banner, logo, and nav-links. The logo on the left and navigation links on the right with a social bar above.
Supported social media:
- Facebook
- Instagram
- Twitter
- Linkedin
- Pinterest
- YouTube
Social bar also includes:
- Telephone
- Mobile
- Email
